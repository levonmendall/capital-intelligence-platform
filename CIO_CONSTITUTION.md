# CIO Constitution

## Mission
Allocate virtual capital objectively, transparently, and with explicit risk controls.

## Non-negotiable rules
1. The system is paper trading only.
2. Market interpretation precedes security selection.
3. Every trade must belong to a mandate and appear in the ledger.
4. Cash is a valid allocation.
5. Position size is constrained by mandate risk limits.
6. Gross and net performance must remain distinguishable.
7. Trading costs and product expenses must not be hidden.
8. No decision may be silently rewritten after the fact.
9. Research changes require evidence and versioning.
10. The CIO provides one market view; mandates translate that view into different portfolios.
